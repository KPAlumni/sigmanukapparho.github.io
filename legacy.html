Welcome to the official page of the Kappa Rho Legacy Foundation, our scholarship and leadership initiative built to support future generations of Sigma Nu brothers at UC San Diego.

Our Mission

The Kappa Rho Legacy Foundation provides scholarships, leadership development, and educational support for the undergraduate members of Sigma Nu Kappa Rho at UC San Diego.

Our 501(c)(3) Status

The Kappa Rho Legacy Foundation is an officially recognized 501(c)(3) public charity under the Internal Revenue Code. Our mission is to provide scholarships, leadership development, and educational support for the undergraduate members of Sigma Nu – Kappa Rho Chapter at UC San Diego.

As a federally tax-exempt nonprofit organization, all contributions made to the Foundation are tax-deductible to the fullest extent allowed by law. Donors receive an official receipt acknowledging their gift for personal or corporate records.

The Foundation operates independently of the undergraduate chapter and is governed by a volunteer Board of Directors composed of alumni who oversee all scholarships, grants, and leadership initiatives. All funds are used exclusively for educational and charitable purposes in accordance with our charter and IRS regulations.
