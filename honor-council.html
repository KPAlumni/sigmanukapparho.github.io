Honor Council

Sigma Nu Fraternity — Kappa Rho Chapter

Purpose

The Honor Council is responsible for upholding the standards, values, and expectations of Sigma Nu through fair, confidential, and consistent judicial review.

Authority & Scope

  • Adjudicates alleged violations of chapter bylaws and codes of conduct
  • Ensures due process and procedural fairness
  • Recommends and enforces sanctions when applicable
  • Maintains confidentiality and accurate records

Structure

  • Chair
  • Voting Members
  • Recording Secretary
  • Advisor (non-voting)

Judicial Process Overview

  1. Case referral or intake
  2. Preliminary review
  3. Hearing and fact-finding
  4. Deliberation
  5. Determination and sanctions (if applicable)
  6. Notification and appeals

Meetings

Honor Council meetings are held as needed. Quorum is required for all determinations. Portions of meetings may be closed to preserve confidentiality.
