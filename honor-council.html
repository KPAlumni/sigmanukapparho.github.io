Honor Council

The Honor Council of Sigma Nu Kappa Rho is responsible for upholding the chapter’s standards through fair, confidential, and consistent judicial review.

Purpose

The Honor Council exists to preserve the integrity of the chapter by addressing conduct matters with due process, impartiality, and respect for all individuals involved.

Authority & Scope

  • Adjudicates alleged violations of chapter bylaws and codes of conduct
  • Ensures procedural fairness and confidentiality
  • Determines responsibility and applies sanctions when appropriate
  • Maintains accurate and secure records

Structure

  • Chair
  • Voting Members
  • Recording Secretary
  • Advisor (non-voting)

Judicial Process Overview

  1. Case referral or intake
  2. Preliminary review
  3. Hearing and fact-finding
  4. Deliberation
  5. Determination and sanctions (if applicable)
  6. Notification and appeals
