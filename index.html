The Weekend at a Glance

Join us for a celebration of brotherhood, legacy, and the 1000th member initiation. Below you'll find a detailed itinerary for the 3-day celebration, with each day bringing something special.

Celebrating 40 Years of Brotherhood

Join us for a once-in-a-lifetime celebration as we honor the legacy of Sigma Nu Kappa Rho, commemorate our 1000th initiated member, and reconnect with generations of alumni.

Date: October 17-20th, 2025
Location: UC San Diego
Attire: Cocktail / Business Formal

Friday – October 17th

📍 Dirty Birds (formerly Round Table Pizza) 🕒 3:00pm – 7:00pm

Start the weekend at Dirty Birds, where you’ll check in and reconnect with brothers. That evening, head to the Chapter House for a classic Beirut Night and casual kickoff to the celebration.

📍 Chapter House 🕗 8:00pm – Late

Drinks, games, and stories under the roof that started it all.

Saturday – October 18th

📍 UCSD Campus 🕙 10:00am – 12:00pm

Secondary check-in and our annual Active/Alumni Chapter Meeting. Then enjoy an alumni pub crawl starting from Dirty Birds.

📍 Dirty Birds (Pub Crawl Start) 🕛 12:00pm – 3:00pm

A spirited afternoon of drinks and memories with brothers across the generations.

📍 Shores Diner (formerly Porter’s Pub) 🕠 5:30pm – 9:00pm

Cocktail reception open to all guests and plus-ones — a celebratory night at the heart of UCSD.

Sunday – October 19th

📍 La Jolla Shores Park 🕚 11:00am – 2:00pm

Close out the weekend with a beachfront BBQ brunch for the whole family. Good food, goodbyes, and plenty of photo ops by the ocean.
