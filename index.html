Celebrating 40 Years of Brotherhood

Join us for a once-in-a-lifetime celebration as we honor the legacy of Sigma Nu Kappa Rho, commemorate our 1000th initiated member, and reconnect with generations of alumni. See the detailing of events below.

📅 October 17–19, 2025
📍 UC San Diego

Reserve Your Spot

Friday – October 17th

Check In & Kickoff

📍 Dirty Birds (formerly Round Table Pizza) 🕒 3:00pm – 7:00pm

Join us as we kick off the weekend in SNU territory with an afternoon of wings, brotherhood, and beer at Dirty Birds (formerly Round Table Pizza). Come and check in to receive your commemorative Kappa Rho merchandise and wristband (see below), which will serve as your ticket to all events throughout the weekend.

Beirut Night

📍 Chapter House 🕗 8:00pm – ???

After the buzz of old stories and booze has set in, join us as we make our way to a chapter house to re-live and reinvigoroute your youth. Test your Beirut skills against the champions of today and the legends of the past as we come together and enjoy the debauchery of Kappa Rho's signature pastime.

Saturday – October 18th

Secondary Check In

📍 UCSD Lecture Hall 🕙 10:00am – 11:00am

If you missed primary check in on day one, or are arriving later, come by early to our meeting lecture hall (exact location TBD) to grab your merchandise and ticket. Take some time to mingle and catch up with old friends before we begin the programming of the day.

Active/Alumni Chapter Meeting

📍 UCSD Lecture Hall 🕙 11:00am – 12:00pm

Join as us we come together as both the active and alumni chapters to conduct an all-chapter meeting. Throw yourself back into the ritual and tradition never forgotten from your days as an active member. Write some brother zeros for those brothers who haven't caught enough heat for the mischief they've been up to post-grad, and catch up on what the active chapter is currently working on.

Pub Crawl

📍 Dirty Birds → Ridgewalk Social → Shores Diner 🕛 12:00pm – 3:00pm

Following our meeting, raise a glass on a nostalgic pub crawl through campus favorites, or new spots you've never seen before. Share stories, reconnect with old friends, or meet new ones as we spend an hour each at Dirty Birds, Ridgewalk Social, and Shores Diner.

Cocktail Reception

📍 Shores Diner (formerly Porter’s Pub) 🕠 5:30pm – 9:00pm

After recovering from the events of the afternoon, the active chapter of Kappa Rho is pleased to invite you to our cornerstone event of the weekend. Just behind Shores Diner (formerly known as Porter's Pub).

Sunday – October 19th

Farewell Barbeque

📍 La Jolla Shores Park 🕚 11:00am – 2:00pm

One last hug, one last plate, and a group photo by the sea. Bring the whole family for brunch on the beach as we close out 40 unforgettable years.
