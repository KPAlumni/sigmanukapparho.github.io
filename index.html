Kappa Rho 40th Anniversary

The Weekend at a Glance

Join us for a celebration of brotherhood, legacy, and the 1000th member initiation. Below you'll find a detailed itinerary for the 3-day celebration, with each day bringing something special.

Celebrating 40 Years of Brotherhood

Join us for a once-in-a-lifetime celebration as we honor the legacy of Sigma Nu Kappa Rho, commemorate our 1000th initiated member, and reconnect with generations of alumni.

Date: October 17-20th, 2025
Location: UC San Diego
Attire: Cocktail / Business Formal

Friday – Welcome Reception

Location: UC San Diego Faculty Club

Time: 6:00pm – 10:00pm

Enjoy hors d’oeuvres, wine tasting, and reconnecting with brothers from across the years.

Saturday – Legacy Banquet

Location: UCSD Atkinson Pavilion

Time: 5:00pm – 10:00pm

Formal dinner, award presentations, and the ceremonial honoring of our 1000th initiated brother.

Sunday – Brotherhood Brunch

Location: La Jolla Shores Park

Time: 10:00am – 1:00pm

Bring the whole family for catered brunch and farewell photos at the beach.
